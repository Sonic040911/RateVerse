Support & FAQ

Find answers to commonly asked questions or contact our support team for assistance.

General
Account & Technical
Ratings & Lists
What is RateVerse?
RateVerse is an interactive platform created for those who value opinions and seek to share their ratings. Our site provides the opportunity to both browse existing ratings across various categories and create your own.
Is RateVerse free to use?
Yes, RateVerse is completely free to use. You can browse ratings, create ratings, and leave your own ratings without any cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.
How can I explore ratings without an account?
You can browse ratings, search for content, and view categories without an account. However, to rate items, create lists, or leave reviews, you'll need to sign up by clicking the profile icon and selecting 'Sign Up'.
What type of content can I rate on RateVerse?
RateVerse supports ratings for a wide variety of content including movies, TV shows, games, books, music, and more. We're constantly expanding our categories based on user feedback and demand.
How do I create an account?
To create an account, click the Sign Up button in the top right corner of the Home page. Follow the prompts to enter your email, phone number, create a username and password.
Can I change my username?
Yes, you can change your name. To do this, click on the profile icon at the top right of the page, then click the "My Profile" button to go to your profile page. On your profile page, click the pencil icon to edit your name.
How do I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, click on "Login" and then select "Forgot Password". Enter the email associated with your account, and we'll send you instructions on how to reset your password.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to your profile settings and select "Account". At the bottom of the page, you'll find the option to "Delete Account". Please note that this action is permanent and will remove all your ratings, reviews, and lists.
Is there a mobile app for RateVerse?
We are currently developing mobile apps for iOS and Android. In the meantime, our website is fully responsive and works well on mobile devices.
The site is running slowly. What can I do?
If the site is running slowly, try clearing your browser cache and cookies. If the problem persists, it could be due to a slow internet connection or temporary server issues. Please try again later or contact support if the issue continues.
How do I rate content?
To rate content, go to the detailed page of the rating you want to evaluate. On that page, you can choose from 1 to 5 stars to give your rating. You can also write a comment to explain why you gave that rating or share your opinion.
How do I create a rating list?
To create a rating list, go to the Ratings page and click the + button in the bottom right corner. Then click the "Create New List" button. You can create a new rating list by entering a title and description, and uploading an image.
How do I share a list?
To share your list, go to the Feedback page and click the "Share" button. You can copy the direct link or share it on social media.
How to enable dark mode?
To change the background color, you can click on the profile icon in the top right corner of the page and then click the "Theme" button to make the change.
How do I delete a list?
To delete a list, go to the Create page, click on your created list under "Your objects", and delete it by clicking the "Delete" button.
